What is GDRRC

The GDRRC is a central informational resource on international and domestic alternative dispute resolution (ADR) initiatives for academics and practitioners worldwide. By sharing knowledge and experience, it aspires to promote the appropriate use and institutionalization of ADR processes compatible with justice, economic opportunity, and human dignity through the rule of law. The GDRRC is designed to promote a community of practice through crowdsourcing relevant information, providing social networking, posting an events calendar, and encouraging engagement through a blog.
