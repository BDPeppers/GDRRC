The ADR Mapping Project compiles domestic laws on arbitration and mediation and organizes them by country. This information may be useful to academics for comparative study, practitioners for reference and compliance, and policymakers who are developing new legislation and institutionalizing ADR in their respective legal systems and social institutions. Country information also includes links to country-specific organizations (both governmental and NGOs) that promote ADR and provide ADR services.
